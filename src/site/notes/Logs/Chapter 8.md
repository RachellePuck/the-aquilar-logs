The start of the day is marked by a lecture on the Red Garden, one of the first ruins discovered in the New Horizon, consisting of many different ruins. It is also the namesake of the garden type classification of ruins. One of the lecturers is a member of the [[Guilds/Explorers Guild\|Explorers Guild]], Shamran. It is also knows as the Crimson Forest. This garden is actually not just a garden, but it is the garden of the Judge. The explorer also mentions there are guided tours, which [[Characters/Ava Aquilar\|Ava]] has led a few of in the past.

During the explorer's lecture Ava gives later, they see a pinprick of light, unsure of whether it's a star. 

---

During dinner the group is joined by [[Atapila\|Atapila]] who is very enthusiastic about [[Characters/Dorian Golgayad\|Dorian]] joining more of the masses. He warns [[Characters/Dorian Golgayad\|Dorian]] to not turn into a man without perspective. After a bit of talking about purpose, [[Characters/Ava Aquilar\|Ava]] asks [[Characters/Dorian Golgayad\|Dorian]] what he plans to do once they get to [[Locations/Gamaru\|Gamaru]], and offers the possibility for him to join them on some small exploration into surface ruins. He happily accepts, and [[Characters/Veronica Buttons\|Veronica]] decides to join as well.

Mass later is on the Dancer, with a lot singing to 'sing the troubles of the day away'

---

As she sleeps, [[Characters/Ava Aquilar\|Ava]] finds herself in the Halls. There is almost a sensory deprivation, not feeling her own touch, or any sensory feedback. She takes some time to orient herself, being quite close to where she heard the wind, the last time she was there. She tries to find her way back to that place, starting to hear the sound of wind, like a soft breeze that becomes louder and louder, and she approaches the grand archway. [[Characters/Ava Aquilar\|Ava]] start heading towards the gate, almost a tunnel or corridor. The sound of the wind becomes louder and louder, starting to sting in her ears. She pushes through, feeling like she could faint from the endless banging on her eardrums. Her consciousness starts to fade but not before she can see a hand reaching out to her through the faded opening at the end of the tunnel. This could mean she's not alone here. 

She wakes up with blood on her pillow, and a splitting migraine.

---

In the Dining Hull, [[Characters/Veronica Buttons\|Veronica]] helps [[Characters/Ava Aquilar\|Ava]] map out the hallways she traversed in her dream through vague instructions. The others join, seeing [[Characters/Ava Aquilar\|Ava]] look rough, and wonder if she should go to the medbay. The others also talked about [[Characters/Selene Dara\|Selene]]'s grandfather for a bit, and Veronica implied that somehow the first officer of this ship should know her grandfather since he was also first officer of a ship.

While [[Characters/Ava Aquilar\|Ava]] goes to sleep off the migraine, the others join a lecture on Amalum, the gas giant the Red Garden orbits around. It has rings, and sometimes scavengers find free-floating artifacts in them. There is an outpost around there and there's a phenomenon known as Gas Wights, which are gas formed appendages that lash out around it, costing several hundred lives already. Since the moon that orbits it is called the Garden of the Judge, these Gas Wights are known as the Lashes of the Judge.

During the following week, the entire group takes [[Characters/Ava Aquilar\|Ava]]'s theoretical exam and all but [[NPC's/Ship City/Clemence Dukus\|Clemence]] succeed and learn a lot about ruins and the Builders. [[NPC's/Ship City/Clemence Dukus\|Clemence]] does finally manage to pass his physical exam. They celebrate with virgin cocktails.

Also during this time, [[Characters/Selene Dara\|Selene]] notices that between the people on the ship, a lot of cliques are forming and notes are passed between the passengers. She manages to snatch one of the notes, which is one side of a conversation about how the Coriolites still don't have a place on the council, and movements have to be made to make change. It's signed "A.S."

[[Characters/Ava Aquilar\|Ava]] and [[Characters/Veronica Buttons\|Veronica]] spend time observing the ship itself. [[Characters/Ava Aquilar\|Ava]] notes this ship is in as good a shape as it can be, and there is very clearly a second ship, a slipstream cruiser, next to them. It is the same kind of make as the greatship, but smaller, with greater mobility, and weaponry to defend. She guesses that someone on the bridge had requested its assistance.

[[Characters/Dorian Golgayad\|Dorian]] is looking out into far space, noting that the small blip they'd seen before has grown a little bigger, getting the feeling that the cruiser escort is not the only thing following [[Locations/The Zalos\|The Zalos]]. Also looking out into the Great Dark, something catches his eye, as he sees a small, translucent pearlescent globule attached to the ship, with two stalks with what look to be eyes. He recognises this as a Slipstream Nieba, also know as Juice Leeches. They are known to siphon power from Greatships and should be contained since they could grow out of proportion. He asks [[Characters/Veronica Buttons\|Veronica]] to draw it before pointing it out to the crew. [[Characters/Dorian Golgayad\|Dorian]] goes to tell the first officer, who immediately shuts off all the power.

[[Characters/Ava Aquilar\|Ava]] recognises this doesn't seem to be an emergency like a hull breach, but leads [[Characters/Selene Dara\|Selene]] and [[NPC's/Ship City/Clemence Dukus\|Clemence]] to the second lounge to find the others. They all see the constables in exosuits, getting ready to eliminate the creature. Seeing the threat, it starts to suck more energy out of the system while the constables start shooting it. It starts to pulsate and quiver rapidly, making the hull shriek and breaking some sort of barrier. One of the constables, whose boots are magnetised to the ship, suddenly goes limp, unconscious. It then shoots out tentacles, crushing another of the constables.
