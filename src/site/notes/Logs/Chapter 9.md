*23-03-2026*

There were a few options to deal with the Nieba, using  [[Characters/Selene Dara\|Selene]]'s ship to ram it, which could cause damage to both ships. Turning off all systems, making the Nieba hopefully lose interes, but this would also turn off all life support systems. The last option would be to overload the system momentarily, trying to explode it, but this could also damage the systems.

They move to the bridge to try and set this last plan in action. Moving to the bridge, they hear people shouting and someone giving orders. Officer Kel comes over and tells them they aren't supposed to be here. They discuss loudly in the hopes that someone with authority over the power systems, which they do. The master engineer comes over and takes them to the captain, and they explain the plan of overloading the ship again. 

Together with the master engineer, [[Characters/Ava Aquilar\|Ava]] calculates the amount of power needed to overload the Nieba, and offers to go outside in an exo-suit to disconnect the life support systems so they won't get damaged. She goes out, and asks [[Characters/Selene Dara\|Selene]] to be on standby in her own ship to catch anyone whose magnetised boots overload, at least two people still were still on the hull, fighting the Nieba.

[[Characters/Ava Aquilar\|Ava]] deftly makes her way to the life support system and disconnects it, starting on her way back, but realising that officer Kel and another were on the hull, so she asked [[Characters/Selene Dara\|Selene]] to intercept them as they floated off into space. She did so, with [[Characters/Veronica Buttons\|Veronica]] on board, but as he floated towards them, he started shooting at the Nieba and it started growing again. As [[Characters/Selene Dara\|Selene]] relayed this, [[Characters/Ava Aquilar\|Ava]] told the engineer to recalculate the power needed to overload it with [[Characters/Dorian Golgayad\|Dorian]]'s help. 

As soon as [[Characters/Ava Aquilar\|Ava]] and [[Characters/Selene Dara\|Selene]] get back inside, the master engineer overcharges the system, and the entire ship is shook by a large popping explosion. Slowly the lights come back on, and the plan seemed to have worked. [[Characters/Ava Aquilar\|Ava]] starts going back outside to reconnect the life support systems, while the others go to the lounges to offer first aid to the Coriolites who were shook by the explosion. 

Outside, Ava fixes life support and moves on to the place on the hull where the Nieba had been. Here she sees a large blackened stain, and some remains of the creature, which she collects in a lantern for [[Characters/Dorian Golgayad\|Dorian]] so he can research it.
