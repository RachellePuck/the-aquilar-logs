Even though the room is very dimly lit, but [[Ammi Alamasy\|Ammi]]'s eyes seem to have turned completely black. She says there are usually two readings, a group reading, or an individual reading. She starts with the group reading, saying she can see they're all looking for something: purpose, family, loved one. They are all looking for the same place. Finding what they are looking for will be difficult with its own taxes and tolls to pay. They would need a navigator.

> 
> Then she looks to [[NPC's/Ship City/Clemence Dukus\|Clemence]], who will drown but not in water.
>

>

They discuss the readings over tea, brought by a servant. [[Characters/Dorian Golgayad\|Dorian]] asks if the [[Lore/The Coriolites/Nazar\|Nazar]] have been bothering her, to which she stiffens and says they have a little. She says they have bad experiences with oracles, their parent having been predicted doom by an oracle and having met that fate when the twins were only 14.

[[Ammi Alamasy\|Ammi]] advices [[Characters/Veronica Buttons\|Veronica]] that if she wants to know the truth, she should visit [[Ammi Alamasy\|Ammi]]'s aunt, Gallatea who has a gallery in Ubarum. She herself will be heading to a further system towards Shybur, hoping that science can help her regain some of her sight.

During [[Characters/Ava Aquilar\|Ava]]'s theory training, which [[Characters/Dorian Golgayad\|Dorian]] and [[Characters/Veronica Buttons\|Veronica]] loosely join, they see people outside, working, with thick exosuits on. 

[[Characters/Dorian Golgayad\|Dorian]] asks if the others have thought about their readings, and they discuss a little more. They note that [[Characters/Dorian Golgayad\|Dorian]]'s reading might be about him questioning the blind faith the Coriolites have in the Icons.

[[Characters/Ava Aquilar\|Ava]] explains about 4 types of ruins:
Structures - a tower or other building above ground
Gardens - vaults that span multiple different buildings but still largely above ground
Shallows - which start to go below ground
Deep Vaults - have been explored but assigned deep vault because the crew didn't come back

A lot of ruins have been mapped and catalogued but not fully explored, a couple of structures hold great names like the ice cathedral, the temple of tomorrow, the Arakvarat tomb portal. The amount of deep vaults is small because an explorer crew has to have died within it to be labeled that. Only 3 known ones. [[Characters/Dorian Golgayad\|Dorian]]'s [[Lore/Garuda\|Garuda]] is from the Great Vault of Xu, which is also a Deep Vault.

The following day [[Characters/Veronica Buttons\|Veronica]] hears footsteps outside her door, then a loud ping and hissing. Following where the sound came from, she can't seem to find the source and starts to get a little woozy. She finds someone who works on the ship and asks him to help find what it is, but this man also cannot find it, both of them getting light-headed. The man leaves to get someone to figure it out and comes back with the master engineer. They enter and start to curse and shout, asking if Veronica has a gun. [[Characters/Veronica Buttons\|Veronica]] is taken in for questioning about damage to the ship.

[[Characters/Veronica Buttons\|Veronica]] is taken up to the bridge, which has a glass dome that shows a 360º view of the universe around them. She gets asked questions about what she was doing, if she was tampering with the ship, since her room was slowly depressurising. She will have to stay out of her room for the day while the engineers fix the damage, and gets a coupon for a free drink in the lounge.

She meets the rest of the group in the dining hull, and explains the situation. [[Characters/Selene Dara\|Selene]] knows of the legend of the Black Bir. A small meteorite that is no larger than a coin, that seems to pierce ships as if they're butter. They talk a little about whether it could be a sign from the Icons, but most likely it's just a coincidence.

They do another theory lesson, and during dinner; they are joined by [[Malik\|Malik]] who seems to be prying into what the group has been doing. He asks [[Characters/Ava Aquilar\|Ava]] if she is an explorer, and asks if, when the opportunity arises, she would take some contract work for him. He doesn't want to run it through the guild since he's a Coriolite. After he's left, [[Characters/Dorian Golgayad\|Dorian]] mentions that he's probably a [[Lore/The Coriolites/Sakaraya\|Sakaraya]]. 

The group decides to join evening Mass, since [[Malik\|Malik]] was enquiring about it and they thought they might stand out with the Coriolites for not joining. This evening is another one on the Traveler, and its darker aspect; The Demi-urge. This sermon claims that the Demi-urge was the creator of the gates and denies the existence of the Builders. It also goes a little bit into the dark aspect of the Traveler, which is the spider. Being warned of the darkness.
